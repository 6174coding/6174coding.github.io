1. Differentiation aka Derivatives

This isn't really calculus, I know, but this is how far I've gotten in math (precalculus). Also, because of limits to how I can input mathematical equations, I can only teach you differentiation. Let's move on to the actual math.

What differentiation is really about is finding out the gradient of a curve at any fixed point that lies on it. Differentiation is usually denoted as dy/dx. Let me provide an example of differentiation. Let's say we have the function y = x^2 + 2x + 1 plotted on a graph.
